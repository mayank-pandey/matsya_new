Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Team

AUV-IITB comprises of highly enthusiastic and hard working technocrats ranging from bright-eyed freshmen
to driven senior undergraduates.The team works towards participating at AUVSI Robosub Competition,
where the skills of underwater robotics are tested. Each year the team strives to build a better AUV with a more efficient design,
which is at par with that of professional research organizations.
